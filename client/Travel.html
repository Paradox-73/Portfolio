Pondicherry

March 2024

Buckle up everyone because, I, Kanav will take you on a whirlwind adventure. Pondicherry, the promise of sun, sand, and a much-needed escape from lectures. We boarded the overnight train. Modi got molested but we don’t talk about it.

group photo

We navigated the streets with four scooters but one license (courtesy of Harsh Gupta ji!). We made a stop at Baker Street. The aroma of freshly baked bread was pure magic. Disaster struck on serenity beach, when I lost Kanav's glasses in the water. Refreshed and ready to hit the town, we donned our coolest attire (shorts and slippers). Clubbing dreams were shattered by the dress code gods, leaving us at Pondy Cafe.

Singapore

July 2024

My first time abroad, and Singapore did not hold back. We crossed the causeway into Malaysia first, then spent days losing ourselves in the city — the roar of Universal Studios, that impossible skyline from Marina Bay Sands, and the glowing Supertrees at Gardens by the Bay after dark.

somewhere across the causeway — Malaysia

Lazy afternoons on Sentosa, and the indoor waterfall at Jewel Changi that made an airport feel like a wonder. The cruise was its own adventure — I tried new cuisines every night and ate my way through what felt like twelve different species of land and sea. We ended, full and happy, in Little India.

the lion city

Goa was the trip that felt like a movie. I finished The Perks of Being a Wallflower on the sand, ate the best pizza of my life at Southern Deck, and on a whim we drove the whole coast — north to south — at two in the morning, windows down. Back at the Airbnb there was the pool we never wanted to leave.

December 2025

We took the Vande Bharat up north — Modi, Batra and Garv famously missed their train, and honestly we almost did too. The 13 km trek up was a quiet test of how fit we actually were (verdict: barely). Pune

April 2025

A trip squeezed into the middle of the semester — but a paper of mine got accepted at a conference, and the college footed the hotel, so we picked one with a pool right in the centre of the city. We ate our way down FC Road (Burger King, Camp, and La Casetta pizza), wandered Shaniwar Wada fort, and slowed down in the old Parsi cafes.
